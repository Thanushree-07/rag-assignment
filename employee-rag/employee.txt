Employee Handbook

Work Hours Policy

The standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Employees are expected to be available during these hours unless prior approval has been granted for flexible scheduling. Overtime must be approved by the reporting manager.

Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Remote work must be approved by the reporting manager. While working remotely, employees must remain reachable through official communication channels and must attend all virtual meetings.

Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 20 paid leave days per year. Leave requests must be submitted at least three days in advance unless in case of emergency. Sick leave exceeding two consecutive days requires valid medical documentation. Unused leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.

Attendance Policy

Employees must log their attendance daily using the company attendance portal. More than three late arrivals in a month may lead to a formal warning. Continuous absenteeism without notice may result in disciplinary action.

IT Usage Policy

Employees must use only company-approved software on organizational systems. Installing unauthorized software or accessing restricted websites is strictly prohibited. All system issues must be reported immediately to the IT helpdesk.

Password and Security Policy

Employees must maintain confidentiality of their login credentials. Sharing passwords is strictly prohibited. Passwords must be updated every 60 days and must contain a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

Code of Conduct

All employees must behave professionally in the workplace. Harassment, discrimination, bullying, or misuse of company resources is strictly prohibited. Violations of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action including termination.

Data Protection Policy

Employees must handle company data responsibly. Confidential information must not be shared with external parties without authorization. Sensitive documents must be stored securely in company-approved systems only.

Equipment Usage Policy

Employees are responsible for any company equipment issued to them. Equipment must be used only for official work purposes. Damage or loss of equipment must be reported immediately.

Performance Evaluation Policy

Employee performance is reviewed every six months. Evaluations are based on productivity, teamwork, communication skills, and adherence to company policies. Managers provide feedback and improvement recommendations during review sessions.
